   184в†’## Domain Context
   185в†’
   186в†’### System Purpose
   187в†’
   188в†’MMS replaces manual Excel workflows with a rule-based, predictive engine that:
   189в†’- Optimizes preventative maintenance scheduling
   190в†’- Balances mileage across the fleet
   191в†’- Provides unified interface between Maintenance and Operations
   192в†’
   193в†’### Key Concepts
   194в†’
   195в†’| Term                  | Description                                   |
   196в†’|-----------------------|-----------------------------------------------|
   197в†’| **Rolling Stock**     | Railway vehicles requiring maintenance        |
   198в†’| **Fleet**             | Group of vehicles sharing maintenance profile |
   199в†’| **Mileage Balancing** | Strategy to equalize wear across fleet        |
   200в†’| **Run Assignment**    | Daily train allocation to routes              |
   201в†’| **PM**                | Preventive Maintenance (PM1, PM2)             |
   202в†’| **HM**                | Heavy Maintenance / Overhaul                  |
   203в†’
   204в†’### Core Modules
   205в†’
   206в†’1. **Maintenance Planning** - Short to long-term maintenance schedules
   207в†’2. **Operation Planning** - Daily fleet deployment optimization
   208в†’3. **Resource Planning** - Costing and resource smoothing
