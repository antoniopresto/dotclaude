     7→## 1. Scope of Work
     8→
     9→Develop a solution that allows the maintenance and operations team to efficiently plan and schedule preventative maintenance activities for **two distinct rolling stock fleets** (49 + 79 = 128 trains) while fulfilling operational requirements across the network.
    10→
    11→The system should offer an **automated, data-driven, and real-time mileage management** approach, considering:
    12→- Different preventive maintenance activities
    13→- Current resources
    14→- Operational needs
    15→
    16→### Requirement Categories
    17→
    18→1. **Maintenance Planning**
    19→2. **Operational Planning**
    20→3. **Resource Planning**
    21→

    60→## 5. Background & Current State
    61→
    62→### Current Pain Points (from RFQ)
    63→
    64→- ❌ No dedicated tools for mileage management
    65→- ❌ IBM Maximo is asset management only, not mileage-specific
    66→- ❌ Mileage managed manually via Excel
    67→- ❌ Raw mileage data stored manually (folder-driven)
    68→- ❌ Limited integration between maintenance and operations
    69→- ❌ Risk of non-compliance due to manual mileage tracking
    70→- ❌ No predictive simulations
    71→- ❌ Insufficient resource visibility vs utilization
    72→- ❌ Manual handling causes delays and inefficiencies
    73→
    74→### Strategic Opportunity
    75→
    76→This demo addresses these pain points with a **"Single Pane of Glass"** visualization that:
    77→
    78→1. Unifies strategic, tactical, and operational views
    79→2. Provides semantic zoom (Google Earth-like UX)
    80→3. Shows conflicts and resource constraints visually
    81→4. Enables future What-If simulation capabilities
