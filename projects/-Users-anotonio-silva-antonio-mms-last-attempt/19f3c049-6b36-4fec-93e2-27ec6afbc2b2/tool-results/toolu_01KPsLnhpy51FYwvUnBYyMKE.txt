    12вҶ’
    13вҶ’### 1.1 Objective
    14вҶ’
    15вҶ’Win the bidding process by demonstrating an exceptional frontend solution that addresses all client pain points from the RFQ. The demo must visually impress while proving we can deliver a production-ready "Single Pane of Glass" for Rolling Stock Maintenance.

    17вҶ’### 1.2 RFQ Pain Points вҶ’ Our Solutions
    18вҶ’
    19вҶ’| # | Client Pain Point (RFQ) | Our Solution | Key Visual Element |
    20вҶ’|---|------------------------|--------------|-------------------|
    21вҶ’| 1 | No dedicated mileage management tools | Purpose-built mileage dashboard | KPI Cards + Mileage indicators |
    22вҶ’| 2 | IBM Maximo only does asset management | Integrated mileage + maintenance planning | Unified Planning View |
    23вҶ’| 3 | Manual Excel management | Interactive visual timeline with drag operations | Gantt-style canvas |
    24вҶ’| 4 | Folder-driven data storage | Centralized fleet visualization | Sidebar + Fleet grid |
    25вҶ’| 5 | Limited maintenance-operations integration | Single pane for both teams | Shared workspace |
    26вҶ’| 6 | Non-compliance risk (underrun/overrun) | Visual alerts + compliance indicators | Color-coded tasks + banners |
    27вҶ’| 7 | **No predictive simulations** | **Sandbox Mode for what-if scenarios** | **PRIMARY FEATURE** |
    28вҶ’| 8 | Insufficient resource visibility | Resource utilization panels | Heatmap + capacity bars |
    29вҶ’| 9 | Manual handling delays | Automated scheduling visualization | Smart conflict detection |

    33вҶ’## 2. Design Philosophy
    34вҶ’
    35вҶ’### 2.1 Inspiration Attribution
    36вҶ’
    37вҶ’We draw from multiple sources, each contributing specific elements:
    38вҶ’
    39вҶ’| Source | What We Take | What We DON'T Take |
    40вҶ’|--------|--------------|-------------------|
    41вҶ’| **Linear App** | Dark mode aesthetic, typography, whitespace, micro-interactions | Sprint/issue tracking patterns (different domain) |
    42вҶ’| **JetBrains Fleet Islands UI** | Modular layout, panel separation, information density | IDE-specific features |
    43вҶ’| **DHTMLX Gantt** | Timeline visualization, task bars, dependency lines | Light theme default |
    44вҶ’| **Railnova Railfleet** | Fleet dashboard patterns, train status indicators | Specific ECM workflows |
    45вҶ’| **deck.gl/Kepler.gl** | GPU-accelerated rendering, smooth transitions | Geospatial focus |
    46вҶ’
    47вҶ’### 2.2 Core Principles
    48вҶ’
    49вҶ’1. **Density with Clarity** (Islands UI) - Pack information without overwhelming
    50вҶ’2. **Dark Mode First** (Linear) - Reduce eye strain, modern aesthetic
    51вҶ’3. **Modular Panels** (Fleet) - Visually separated functional areas
    52вҶ’4. **Direct Manipulation** (Gantt) - Drag to reschedule, resize to extend
    53вҶ’5. **GPU Performance** (deck.gl) - 60fps even with 15k+ tasks
