## pixi-viewport

The main library for viewport pan/zoom functionality with Pixi.js is **pixi-viewport**.

It's a highly configurable viewport/2D camera designed to work with pixi.js. Features include dragging, pinch-to-zoom, mouse wheel zooming, decelerated dragging, follow target, snap to point, snap to zoom, clamping, and bouncing on edges.(no content)The library is MIT licensed (c) 2024 by YOPEY YOPEY LLC and David Figatner.
