     1→# MMS - Metro Mileage Management System
     2→
     3→> Enterprise solution to centralize and automate mileage management for mixed rolling stock fleets (>100 units), bridging Operations and Maintenance via predictive, data-driven planning integrated with EAM.
     4→
     5→---
     6→
     7→## Overview
     8→
     9→MMS replaces manual workflows with a rule-based, predictive engine that:
    10→- Optimizes preventative maintenance scheduling
    11→- Balances mileage across the fleet
    12→- Provides unified interface between Maintenance and Operations teams
    13→

    16→## Domain Glossary
    17→
    18→| Term                    | Definition                                                                                                                     | Type        |
    19→|-------------------------|--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|-------------|
    20→| **Rolling Stock**       | Railway vehicles (trains, carriages) requiring maintenance                                                                     | Entity      |
    21→| **Fleet**               | Group of rolling stock sharing same manufacturer/model and maintenance profile                                                 | Entity      |
    22→| **Maintenance Profile** | Set of PM intervals, overhaul schedules, and resource requirements specific to a fleet type                                    | Entity      |
    23→| **Base Depot**          | Designated maintenance facility for a fleet                                                                                    | Entity      |
    24→| **Train Fitness**       | Binary operational readiness (fit/unfit) determined by Maintenance team or EAM. Unfit trains are excluded from run assignments | State       |
    25→| **Run Assignment**      | Daily allocation of train IDs to routes/network based on fitness and mileage targets                                           | Action      |
    26→| **Mileage Balancing**   | Strategy to equalize wear across fleet, preventing underrun/overrun                                                            | Goal        |
    27→| **EAM**                 | Enterprise Asset Management - external system for asset lifecycle (e.g., IBM Maximo, SAP PM)                                   | Integration |
    28→| **TCO**                 | Total Cost of Ownership over fleet lifecycle                                                                                   | Metric      |

    68→## Core Modules
    69→
    70→### 1. Maintenance Planning
    71→
    72→Generates optimized schedules from short-term to long-term horizons.
    73→
    74→| Horizon    | Scope                     | Objective                              |
    75→|------------|---------------------------|----------------------------------------|
    76→| Short-term | Weekly, monthly schedules | Execution feasibility, resource lock   |
    77→| Long-term  | 1-10 year strategic plans | Budget forecasting, lifecycle strategy |
    78→
    79→**Constraints:**
    80→- Man-hour availability
    81→- Track allocation
    82→- Train availability
    83→
    84→**Outputs:**
    85→- Optimized maintenance schedules
    86→- Warnings for mileage underrun/overrun risks
    87→- Scenario simulation capabilities
    88→
    89→---
    90→
    91→### 2. Operation Planning
    92→
    93→Dynamic assignment engine for daily fleet deployment.
    94→
    95→**Flow:**
    96→1. **Input:** Daily operation requirements (train run IDs, network demand)
    97→2. **Process:** Filters available trains by fitness → Ranks by mileage delta (target vs actual)
    98→3. **Output:** `TrainID` + network/route assignment recommendation
    99→4. **Loop:** Ingests actual daily km to update predictions and trigger corrections
   100→
   101→**Interface:** Unified Maintenance ↔ Ops communication for train fitness and mileage management.
   102→
   103→---
   104→
   105→### 3. Resource Planning
   106→
   107→Strategic module for maintenance costing and resource optimization.
   108→
   109→**Capabilities:**
   110→- Resource smoothing: distributes man-hours and spare parts evenly
   111→- Prevents resource peaks/troughs over planning horizon
   112→- Maintenance costing per fleet based on mileage and plan
   113→
   114→**Fixed Parameters:**
   115→- Manpower availability
   116→- Spare parts costs (material + service)
   117→- Track capacity

   164→## For AI Agents
   165→
   166→**System purpose:** Optimize rolling stock maintenance scheduling and mileage distribution.
   167→
   168→**Optimization objective:** Minimize mileage variance across fleet while respecting maintenance windows and resource constraints.

   173→Fleet (1) ---- (*) Train

   201→### Fixed Parameters (Constraints)
   202→
   203→- Man-hour limits (per depot, per period)
   204→- Track availability (slots per depot)
   205→- Cost baselines (material, service, labor)
   206→
   207→### Configurable Thresholds
   208→
   209→| Parameter                | Default | Description                  |
   210→|--------------------------|---------|------------------------------|
   211→| `mileage_underrun_pct`   | -10%    | Trigger warning below target |
   212→| `mileage_overrun_pct`    | +10%    | Trigger warning above target |
   213→| `planning_horizon_years` | 10      | Long-term planning window    |
   214→
   215→### Business Rules
   216→
   217→- `IF` predicted ≠ actual mileage `THEN` trigger warning + recommend adjustment
   218→- `IF` train approaching underrun/overrun threshold `THEN` trigger warning + corrective guidance
   219→- `IF` train unfit `THEN` exclude from run assignment candidates
   220→- Resource planning must avoid peaks/troughs over planning horizon
   221→
   222→### Constraint Priority (highest to lowest)
   223→
   224→1. **Train Fitness** - Unfit trains never assigned
   225→2. **Maintenance Windows** - Scheduled maintenance blocks availability
   226→3. **Resource Limits** - Man-hours, track slots per depot
   227→4. **Mileage Balance** - Optimize within above constraints
   228→
   229→### Integration Points
   230→
   231→- **Input:** Daily mileage (live), fitness status, operation requirements
   232→- **Output:** Optimized schedules, run assignments, compliance reports
   233→- **External (optional):** EAM sync - work orders, asset master data
