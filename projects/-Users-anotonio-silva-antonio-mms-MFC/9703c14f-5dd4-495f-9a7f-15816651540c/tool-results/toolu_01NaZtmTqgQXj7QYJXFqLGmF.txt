   140→As a **developer**,

   142→So that **I can develop frontend, backend, and solver in a coordinated way**.

   164→
   165→As a **developer**,
   166→I want **the complete PostgreSQL schema with migrations**,
   167→So that **all entities are properly modeled and queryable**.
   168→
   169→**Acceptance Criteria:**
   170→

   172→**When** migrations are applied

   175→- `depots` (id, name, code, lat/lon, capacity_tracks, capacity_manhours)
   176→- `trains` (id, train_id, fleet_id FK, base_depot_id FK, current_km, fitness_status)
   177→- `services` (id, service_code, origin, destination, distance_km, times)
   178→- `allocations` (id, service_id FK, train_id FK, date, status, justifications JSONB)
   179→- `mileage_history` (id, train_id FK, recorded_date, km_value)
   180→- `maintenance_schedules` (id, train_id FK, depot_id FK, type, date, status)
   181→**And** all indexes are created for performance
   182→**And** FK constraints enforce data integrity
