    52‚Üí## The `~` Alias (Critical)
    53‚Üí
    54‚ÜíAll `~/` imports resolve to **SuperApp's code** at runtime:
    55‚Üí
    56‚Üí- `~/store` ‚Üí SuperApp's Zustand store (MANDATORY for store imports)
    57‚Üí- `~/redux/*` ‚Üí Legacy Redux
    58‚Üí- `~/routes/*` ‚Üí Navigation
    59‚Üí
    60‚Üí**Tests only**: `~` maps to `./src/` via babel module-resolver.
