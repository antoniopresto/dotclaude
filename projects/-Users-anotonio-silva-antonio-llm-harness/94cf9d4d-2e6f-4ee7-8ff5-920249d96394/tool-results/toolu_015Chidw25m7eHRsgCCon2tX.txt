   306→**Gap Severity Thresholds:**
   307→- Gap > 500: Comfortable (green)

   309→- -500 <= Gap < 0: Slight overallocation (orange)

   311→
   312→**Comparison Modes:**
   313→- Without Adjustment: Shows predicted workload (unleveled)

   315→- Side-by-Side: Shows both charts stacked
